By popular demand, we introduce the game settings

25.06.2020 v1.1 * New

Now you are not limited by default settings. Choose parameters yourself to play the way that suits you best.

  • You can change the round time and the number of words in the hat
  • You can choose the dictionary: both Russian and English dictionaries are now available
  • We've added a “strict” mode. In this case, editing phase starts immediately after time expiration
  • You can now turn the sound off
  • The English interface you are using is part of the update
  • Whatever happens, you can always finish the game and see the results
