Rules

General

Your objective in the hat game – explain as much words as you can. Usually players use a real hat as a word container. They pull words from it and try to explain them. In the context of the pandemic, it's difficult to get together and play hat. So, we've created a web app, which you can use to play the hat game, staying at home.

How to start?

At first you have to put together a group of hat players. The hat is a game, where you have to communicate a lot. You can use any kind of communication or even meet in real life (but only if the quarantine has ended ;). Choose a key, which will be used for entering the game. Also, some of you can create a room and send its key or link to others. The first one to enter will get an important role to start the game when everyone logs in.

Game round

The main part of the game round is explanation stage.
Two players will be chosen as a current pair, everyone will see it in advance. When both of the players are ready, one of them will be shown a word, which he need to explain to the parthner.
If the word has been explained successfully, you should press the Explained button. The word will be removed from the hat, and the current pair will get one point.
If you have violated one of the rules, you should press Mistake button. The word will be removed too, and the explanation will immidiately stop.
If the word turned out to be difficult, and it's clear, that you won't be able to explain it, you can press the Give up button. We don't advise you to do it, because good hat player will try to explain the word to the end.
Keep in mind time. It is limited, and when it expires, the explanation must be finished. The listener will get a one attempt to guess the last word.

Round editing

When the last word is either guessed or not, the speaker will have an opportunity to edit the results of the round in case of their incorrectness.
When the editing phase is finished, the next round starts.

How to explain a word?

You can fix the rules by yourself (even use pantomime), but here is the list of rules we recommend:

  • You can't use congate words
  • You can't use words, that do not exist
  • You can't use the letters of the word (however, you can use terms such as "beginning of the word")

    • You can find detailed rules there http://thehat.ru/rules/

    Results calculation

    The game ends as soon as there are no any more words in the hat. The result of a player is the sum explained and guessed words.
