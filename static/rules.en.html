Rules

The game is also known as "Alias" among English-speakers, but the name "Hat" will be used here.

General

Your aim in the hat game is to explain as many words as you can. Usually players use a real hat as a word container. They pull out the words and try to explain them. In the context of the pandemic, it's difficult to meet each other and play hat. So we've created this web app to allow you playing remotely.

How to start?

At first you have to assemble a team of hat players. The hat is the game where you have to communicate a lot. You can use any kind of communication or even meet in real life (but only if the quarantine has ended ;). Choose a key which will be used to enter the game. Also, if you feel like a leader of your team, you can create a room and send its key or link to the others. In this case, you will become the room holder and you will be able to do special things: to edit the game settings and to start the game.

Game round

The main part of the game round is an explanation stage.
At this stage, two players are chosen as the current pair (speaker and listener) and everyone can see it in advance. As soon as both of them are ready, the former will be shown a word, which is to be explained to the latter.
If the word has been explained successfully, you should press the Explained button. The word will be removed from the hat and the current pair gets one point.
If you have violated one of the rules while explaining, you should press the Mistake button. The word will be removed too and the explanation stops immediately.
If the word seems to be difficult and you don't want to explain it, you can press the Give up button. We don't recommend this, because a good hat player should fight to the end.
Keep track of time! It is limited, and once it expires, the explanation is to be finished. The listener gets only one attempt to guess the last word.

Round editing

After the last word has been either guessed or not, speaker gets an opportunity to edit the results of the round in case they are wrong.
The next round starts only after the editing stage has been completed.

How to explain a word?

You can use your own rules (even pantomime), but there are the rules which we highly recommend:

  • You can't use congate words
  • You can't use non-existent words
  • You can't use the letters of the word (however, you can use terms such as "beginning of the word")

    • You can find detailed rules there http://thehat.ru/rules/

    Summing up

    The game ends as soon as there are no words in the hat. The result of each player is the sum of explained and guessed words.
